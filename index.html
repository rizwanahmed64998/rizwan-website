About Me

My Journey

My journey started on the football field. From a young age, football was not just a game for me—it was a passion that shaped my discipline, confidence, and determination. Over the years, I participated in numerous tournaments, earned achievements, and gained valuable experiences that helped me grow both as an athlete and as a person.

As a football player, I always understood the importance of proper nutrition and quality food. However, finding meals that were both delicious and suitable for an active lifestyle was often difficult. Instead of searching for the perfect option, I decided to create one myself.

This idea inspired me to launch Champaran Wale, where I combined authentic flavors with my own approach to food customization. My goal was simple: serve food that I would personally enjoy and proudly recommend to others.

Today, alongside running my food business, I spend my time coaching and mentoring young football players because football remains a huge part of my life. Helping children learn the game, improve their skills, and develop confidence gives me immense satisfaction. For me, football is more than a sport—it's a way of inspiring and connecting with people.

Whether on the football field or in the kitchen, I believe in dedication, continuous improvement, and creating value for others.

"From the football field to the kitchen, passion has always been my driving force."
